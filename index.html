How to Use

1

Select नेपाली (ne-NP) from the language dropdown above.

2

Tap the 🎙️ microphone button — your browser may ask for mic permission. Allow it.

3

Speak clearly in Nepali. Words appear in real time as you speak.

4

Tap the mic again to stop. Use the buttons to copy, download, or clear the text.

5

You can also edit the text manually in the box — it is fully editable.
